About

I am a recovering digital media professional with twenty five years experience of working at the cutting edge of digital media. These days I am more interested in AI, web3 and not working.

This blog is nothing more than a place to park my own personal ideas and observations from my working life and is not meant as a reflection on any past or current employers.

The posts are more designed to help me formulate my thoughts than for any reasons of self-promotion but I hope that any readers I may find some value in my ramblings.

Thanks for visiting!

Chris
